1, Drag a Xffect prefab to the scene hierarchy.
2, Assign the Xffect object to be the SpriteMaker Component's "Effect" parameter.
3, Play the scene and click "Preview" button.
4, Adjust the camera and parameters to fit the output rectangle.
5, Click "Capture", then all the textures will be outputed in ".\Output" folder.

NOTE: THIS SCENE DOES NOT SUPPORT RUN IN WEBPLAYER MODE.
